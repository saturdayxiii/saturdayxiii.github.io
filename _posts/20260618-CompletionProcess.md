Completion Process is a visceral celebration of the horrific. Vibrant colors and aggressive marks of graphite, watercolor, and ink collide brutally to scream at the viewer while the figure screams impossibly into the abyss of our world beyond its frame -overwhelmed by the metamorphosis of seeing with new eyes.
