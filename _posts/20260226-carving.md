I carved the name of a helpful friend into my flesh, after they met me after midnight to talk me through a violent depressive episode. The kind of episode where cutting a name into my arm out of gratitude was a happy ending, and now their name is permanently in my skin obscured by a bramble pattern of other scars. 

I haven't told my partner of this episode, as her own mental health might be strained by the tribute. But, Ma'am! You've been growing with me for over a decade. Your presence is intertwined with my soul deeper than any scar tissue.
